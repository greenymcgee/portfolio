## Description

~After an autosave on the edit page (`/posts/[id]/edit`), both `/posts/[id]`
and `/posts` show stale content. Neither page reflects the updated title or
content saved by `updatePost`.~

The decision was made to rename `updatePost` to `autosavePost` and to not invalidate the cache after the autosave.
